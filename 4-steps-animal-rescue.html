4 Steps to Animal Rescue

Is the animal...

  • clearly injured?

    • Call to speak with one of our staff in our wildlife rehab unit at (937) 698-6493.

  • sick or acting strangely?

    • Call your county County Wildlife Officer or police non-emergency number.

  • an uninjured nestling?

    • Simply return the young animal to its nest.

  • an older uninjured youngster?

    • Please leave it alone and keep pets and children away so it isn't scared!


Many wild animals are taken from their homes in error. If you have any questions concerning wildlife in need, please call Brukner Nature Center at 937-698-6493 first! We are available 7 days a week to answer your calls. If you ar calling after 5PM, you will be directed to call our emergency after hours advice line. You can also click HERE to search for a licensed wildlife rehabilitator or County Wildlife Officer in your area!
