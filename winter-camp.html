Winter Vacation Camps

Monday, December 21 & Tuesday, December 22

When the air turns frigid and the frost settles on the windows, children fill with anticipation of winter vacation. Sign your child up for a morning of exploration and fill their new found free time with fun and learning. During this winter vacation we will investigate the truth behind some popular wildlife myths! Do all birds migrate for winter? Do squirrels really hibernate? Come meet our wildlife ambassadors and learn what's fact and what's fiction!

Register for one or both days:

  • Monday's topic will be mammals!
  • Tuesday's topic will be birds!

Price per day of Camp:

  • $15 for BNC Members
  • $20 for non-members

Cash or check only please

Deadline: Wednesday, December 16 by 5:00PM
